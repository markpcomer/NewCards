The Martini

  • GIN ~ 3.5oz
  • DRY VERMOUTH ~ Light Splash
  • Olives or Lemon Twist
  • Add GIN and DRY VERMOUTH to mixing glass. Stir with elegant vigor. Strain into chilled coup and add garnish.
